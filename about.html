About Us
Hi everyone!.

We created this website because we feel we have so much information to share when it comes to travel. We have over 20 years in the travel industry working for several airlines and a luxury hotel operator with properties in places such as Dubai, Mexico, Maldives and the Bahamas. So, we have traveled to lots of places. We started off recommending and booking travel for friends and family just for fun because they thought that because we had traveled so much we had "inside information". Which we sorta do. Since we have done so much research over the years, we have acquired a lot of travel knowledge. So recently, we decided to start this blog and a small travel agency to help others find great travel deals. For example, did you know that although most agencies offer travel related deals at the same price, some agencies offer additional incentives to entice you to book? Some incentives include additional on-board credits on cruises, resort and dining credits, guaranteed late checkouts and cash back offers. Some websites advertise incentives that make you think you are getting an exclusive deal when you aren't. They may offer something like free hotel cancellation, which is actually the norm for most hotels these days up to a few days before travel so it doesn't work out to be an exclusive deal. These are just some of the little things we have learned and we want to pass this knowledge on to you.
