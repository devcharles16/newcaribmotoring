American Express - Check out the Benefits

  • by Admin
  • 3 min read

Depending on which American Express Card you have, you'll have access to some of the best benefits when it comes to travel. American Express offers other resort and airline-related credits, car rental insurance, concierge service, and much more. Here is a list of benefits you receive with American Express Green, Gold, and Platinum Cards. Visit American Express for the most accurate and up to date benefits information.

Compare Card Benefits

Green

GoodDeal
  • 3X points on Restaurants, Rideshares and Travel (From flights and rental cars to hotels and home shares)
  • Earn additional points while on vacation
  • Access to the Global Assist Hotline 24/7. They can help you prepare for your trip with information about your destination. If you're 100 miles or more from home, they can coordinate and assist with services like lost passports, translation services, missing luggage, and emergency legal and medical referrals
  • Planned payment options so you can pay for your vacation over time
  • No foreign transaction fees
  • Pay for your vacation with your available points

Gold

BetterDeal
  • 4X points on restaurants and supermarkets(up to $25k per year)
  • 3X points on flights booked directly with airlines or on amextravel.com. So, you can earn additional points while on vacation
  • $120 Dining Credit - Earn up to $10 in credits monthly when you pay at participating partners saving up to $120
  • Book The Hotel Collection† with American Express Travel and stay at least 2 nights, and get a $100 onsite credit for activities like dining or spa treatments
  • Access the Global Assist Hotline 24/7. They can help you prepare for your trip with information about your destination. And, if you're 100 miles or more from home, they can coordinate and assist with services like lost passports, translations, lost luggage, and emergency legal and medical referrals
  • Planned payment options so you can pay for your vacation over time
  • No foreign transaction fees
  • Pay for your vacation with your available points

Platinum

BestDeal
  • 5X points for flights booked directly with airlines or with American Express Travel
  • 5X points on prepaid hotels booked on amextravel.com
  • Access to the Premium Global Assist Hotline 24/7. They can help you prepare for your trip with information about your destination. Also, if you're 100 miles or more from home,they can coordinate and assist with services like lost passports, translation services, missing luggage,and emergency legal and medical referrals are only a phone call away. Plus, they may provide emergency medical transportation assistance and related services.
  • Return Protection - If you try to return an eligible item within 90 days from the date of purchase and the merchant won't take it back, they may refund you minus shipping and handling, up to $300 per item, up to a maximum of $1,000 per year, if you purchased it all on your card.
  • Unlimited American Express lounge visits with up to 2 guests per visit
  • With your complimentary Hilton Honors Card, you can enjoy 10 free lounge visits every year once enrolled.
  • Receive unlimited access to The Centurion Lounge.
  • Get access to 1,200+ lounges in 500+ airports with Global Lounge access.
  • Concierge staff available to you day or night as a resource. Whether you need to select a restaurant and arrange the details of an upcoming special event or if you want expert advice choosing just the right gift, you can rely on Concierge to coordinate and take care of your requests, big and small.
  • When you're ready to relax and need an escape, enjoy an unforgettable experience with Fine Hotels & Resorts®. Book with American Express Travel and you'll receive an average total value of $550 in perks, like daily breakfast for two, 4pm check-out, and more. Plus, book on amextravel.com, and you can earn 5X Membership Rewards® points, or use Pay with Points on prepaid stays.
  • Book The Hotel Collection† with American Express Travel and stay at least 2 nights, and get a $100 onsite credit for activities like dining or spa treatments
  • Get up to $100 in statement credits annually for purchases at Saks Fifth Avenue on your Platinum Card so you can shop for all your vacation gear.
  • No foreign transaction fees
  • Pay for your vacation with your available points
